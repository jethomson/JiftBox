JPEG images and animated GIFs are supported.
JPEG JPEG images will be resized, rotated, and cropped automatically. For best results and to save space you should resize your images to a width of $MY_WIDTH$ and a height of $MY_HEIGHT$.
GIFs Instructions on how to make a compatible GIF:
  1. Go to ezgif.com
  2. Click "Crop"
  3. Upload a GIF
  4. Pick the aspect ratio that matches your display ($MY_RATIO$)
  5. Frame the portion of the GIF you want to keep using your mouse
  6. Click "Crop image!"
  7. Click "resize" under the resulting GIF
  8. Enter the width ($MY_WIDTH$) and height ($MY_HEIGHT$) of your display
  9. Click "Resize image!"
  10. Click "save" under the resulting GIF
  11. Rename your GIF
  12. Put your GIF in a folder
  13. Click "Upload Files" on this page
  14. Click "Browse" and select your GIF's folder
  15. Click "Upload"
  16. Wait for the upload to finish
  17. Press the reset button on the device
